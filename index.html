Who is this Crazy Lady and Why is She Here?

Nycole was born under a full moon on a cold winter day. Since then, she has been on a mission to become a Jill- of-All Tades and gain enough knowledge for world domination.

She is highly creative and can not help thinking outside the box. This often confuses the locals and scares small children.

By default, she is not the sharpest knife in the box, and therefore, has 1,000,000,000,000.5 questions.

Nycole is a person to be feared.

Nycole is fiercely loyal and is not above throwing hands when push comes to shove.

Nycole has been known to knock down small children and the elderly if they dare look at her in any sort of way.

She does not seek forgiveness.
